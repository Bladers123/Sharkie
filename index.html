Game Instruction

In this game, you control Sharkie the fish, whose mission is to navigate through the underwater world to defeat the boss, Vortexia. To win the game, guide Sharkie past obstacles and enemies, collect power-ups to strengthen your abilities, and ultimately confront and defeat Vortexia in the final battle. Victory over Vortexia means victory in the game. Swim carefully, attack wisely, and good luck on your quest!

Turn your Device to play!
